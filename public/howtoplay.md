# How to Play

NOTE: in this article we provide specific instructions on how to play using
[MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), but EthernaLotto supports all the most popular wallets and
connection methods, not just MetaMask.

## Introduction

EthernaLotto is a global, decentralized, autonomous, and most importantly **provably fair** lottery.

## 1. Install and Connect MetaMask

You can skip this step if you already use one of the other supported connection methods, which
include: WalletConnect, Coinbase, Fortmatic, and Portis.

Head on to [metamask.io](https://metamask.io/) and download the extension for your browser.

Once installed, MetaMask will guide you through the creation of a new wallet and provide an option
to buy your first ethers.

## 2. Connect MetaMask

Now you can find and click the "Connect Wallet" button at the top-right corner of the website to
connect MetaMask to EthernaLotto:

This will allow EthernaLotto to buy your lottery tickets using MetaMask (confirmations will be asked
first).

## 3. Select Your Numbers

You can select the numbers you want to play on most pages in this site:

TODO: screenshot of the picker.

You can select at least 6 numbers and at most 20. The price of your ticket will increase based on
how many numbers you are playing. A ticket for 6 numbers has a MATIC base price that is equivalent
to 1.50 US Dollars (updated at every draw based on the latest MATIC market price), and the price of
other tickets is multiplied by the number of possible 6-number combinations that you are playing.

After selecting the numbers you want to play, hit the "Play" button!

MetaMask will show a pop-up window containing various details about the transaction, including the
price of your ticket in MATIC as well as an estimated cap on the gas fee you will pay.

## 5. Wait for the Next Draw

Head on to the ["Past Draws"](/) page. The jackpot and the "draw" tiles at the bottom are updated in
real time. We draw new numbers on Saturday every week (the precise time may change but we will draw
by the end of Saturday in UTC).

If luck is on your side and you matched at least 2 of the drawn numbers with any ticket bought
throughout the last week, you will see something like this:

If you know you have played a ticket that matches 2 or more numbers but your win is not showing up,
please make sure the website is connected to your wallet (refresh the page and click the "Connect
Wallet" button in the top-right corner) and that you are connected to the Matic network.

## 6. Cash Out!

Have you won? That's grand! Visit [ethernalotto.com](https://ethernalotto.com/) at any time, connect
your wallet, click on the tile of your lucky draw, and withdraw your prize by clicking the
"Withdraw" button!

There is no deadline, the smartcontract will keep all your prizes indefinitely until you withdraw
them.
